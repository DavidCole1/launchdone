The process

From your first conversation to a fully operating business — here's exactly what happens.

Six steps. 7–10 business days. Everything built, handed over, and yours.

1

Free — No commitment

Free Strategy Call — 30 minutes

Tell us about your business. We assess where you are, what you need, and which package makes sense. We answer every question. No pitch, no pressure — if we're not the right fit, we'll tell you.

2

Day 1

You Sign and We Start

Sign the service agreement, pay the flat fee, and fill out our 20–30 minute intake questionnaire. That's the last thing we ask of you until delivery. No back-and-forth, no meetings, no tasks.

3

Days 2–8

We Build Everything

Business plan, legal documents, operating systems, brand identity, website, and marketing plan — all built simultaneously by our team. You'll receive a Day 4 progress update so you always know where things stand.

4

Day 9

You Review and Approve

We deliver everything in a shared folder with a full video walkthrough explaining every deliverable. One round of revisions is included. Most clients have minimal changes — because we ask the right questions upfront.

5

Day 10

You Launch

Everything is finalized, published, and handed over. Every document, file, login, and password is transferred to you. Your business is legally registered, operationally ready, and live online.

6

Ongoing

We Stay in Your Corner

The monthly retainer keeps a consultant in your corner as your business grows — strategy calls, content creation, website updates, and direct email access. You never have to figure it out alone.
