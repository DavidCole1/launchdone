About LaunchDone

We exist for the people who are great at what they do — and just need someone to handle the business side.

Our story

Why LaunchDone exists

Most people who start a business are great at what they do — but no one teaches you how to show up online. New business owners spend weeks wrestling with websites, logos, and social media instead of doing the work they love.

LaunchDone exists because that delay costs real people real money. Every week without a professional online presence is a week your competitors are capturing customers you should have had.

We take everything off your plate — branding, website, social profiles, email, and marketing — and hand it back to you in seven days. Done properly. So you can open for business and get to work.

What we believe

Your first impression online should be as good as the work you do in person.

You've spent years getting good at your craft. You show up on time, you do the work right, and your clients rave about you. That same professionalism deserves to be reflected the moment someone finds you online — before they ever pick up the phone. We make sure it is.
