FAQ

Common questions

Do I need my business idea fully figured out?

No. We work with people at every stage — from a napkin concept to an existing business that needs restructuring. Part of what we do is help you pressure-test and refine the idea before we build anything.

How is this different from hiring a lawyer or accountant?

Lawyers handle legal. Accountants handle taxes. Nobody puts it all together and actually builds the business alongside you. LaunchDone covers the full picture — legal, operational, brand, digital, and marketing — coordinated as one project, not five separate engagements.

Can you help an existing business, not just startups?

Yes — our Growth package is specifically designed for established businesses that need a full audit, restructuring, or a strategy to scale. We work with businesses at every stage, not just those starting from zero.

Do I own everything you create?

Yes. Completely. Every document, file, account, login, and deliverable is transferred to you on Day 10. No lock-in, no licensing, no strings. If you ever leave, you leave with everything we built.

What if I need changes after delivery?

That's exactly what the monthly retainer is for. Every package includes ongoing support — strategy calls, content, updates, and direct access to your consultant — so your business keeps getting better after launch, not just on Day 10.
